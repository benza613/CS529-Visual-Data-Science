Slate Gun Deaths Visualization

Welcome to my page, where you will be able to visualize the number of deaths across various states and cities across the US from Dec 2012 to Dec 2013.

  • On Page load, the data is loaded onto a chloropleth map showing the total number of deaths across the various states. A darker shade is indicative of greater number of deaths & victims.
  • Hovering over any state will give you a breakdown of deaths for that state gender-wise.
  • Clicking on any state will:
    1. update the datatable to provide a drilldown of deaths for cities within that particular state across various age groups
    2. also update the overall figures in the totals banner
    3. will provide a simple visualization of the victims that if you click on it, will lead you to the URL sourced for that victim. (unfortunately might take a few seconds to load)
